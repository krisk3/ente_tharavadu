Welcome to

Ente Tharavadu

At Ente Tharavadu, we bring the soul of Kerala to your plate. Rooted in tradition and enriched with generations of culinary wisdom, we specialize in authentic Kerala cuisine, thoughtfully prepared with high-quality ingredients and a deep respect for your health and well-being. Our dishes are crafted by dedicated teams for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian offerings, ensuring every meal reflects the richness of our culture, the purity of our values, and the warmth of heartfelt hospitality.

Our Story

Catering is not just our profession — it’s our heritage. The story of Ente Tharavadu begins decades ago with a family passionate about food. Our director, a third-generation caterer, inherited the art and science of catering from his father and grandfather, both renowned names in the culinary space. Building on this rich legacy, he also pursued formal training in catering to bring together the best of tradition and modern expertise.
