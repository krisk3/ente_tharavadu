About Us

Ente Tharavadu is a third-generation catering company rooted in Kerala’s rich culinary heritage, offering authentic, high-quality cuisine prepared with pure ingredients and a commitment to health. From weddings to corporate events, we deliver wholesome, flavorful food crafted with care and tradition.

Flavours for

Every Occasion

At Ente Tharavadu, we understand that every event is unique—and so is the food that makes it memorable. Whether it's a grand wedding or an intimate social gathering, our team brings unmatched culinary expertise and seamless service to the table.

Weddings

Celebrate your special day with the richness of Kerala’s traditions. From elaborate sadyas to exquisite non-vegetarian feasts, we craft wedding menus that reflect your tastes and wow your guests. Every detail is handled with love, just like family.

Corporate Events

Impress clients and colleagues with professionally curated menus that blend taste with presentation. Whether it’s a formal luncheon or a team celebration, we deliver food that fuels connections and leaves a lasting impression.

Cultural Events

We proudly serve community functions and other cultural gatherings where food plays a central role. Our experience in traditional Kerala cuisine ensures authenticity, precision, and reverence to every dish we serve.

Social Gatherings

Be it a birthday, anniversary, housewarming, or any special get-together, we bring the flavors of home and heritage to your event. You focus on your guests—we’ll take care of the feast.

Why

Choose Us

At Ente Tharavadu, we go beyond just catering
We create experiences rooted in authenticity, quality, and care. Here’s what sets us apart:

Pure Ingredients

We believe great food begins with honest ingredients. That’s why we use only fresh, high-quality produce and never compromise with additives like MSG, artificial colors, or low-grade oils. Health and taste go hand in hand at Ente Tharavadu.

Exotic Cuisine

From timeless Kerala classics to a variety of regional and international dishes, our menus are as diverse as your occasions. Whether it’s a traditional sadya or a custom multi-cuisine spread, we craft every meal with detail and passion.

Experienced Chefs

Our team includes chefs trained in both traditional and contemporary culinary arts. Guided by a legacy of catering and formal expertise, we ensure every dish is a perfect blend of taste, texture, and presentation.

Happy Clients

Our success lies in the smiles of our clients and their guests. With personalized service and consistent quality, we’ve earned the trust of countless families, corporates, and communities across Kerala.
